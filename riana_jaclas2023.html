Riana Presents Study at JACLaS Expo 2023

Last October 6, 2023, Riana Ashley Bautista presented her research to attendees of the JACLaS Expo 2023 held in Yokohama, Japan. Her study entitled "The Internal and External Risk Factors Related to E-Cigarette Smoking Initiation Among Selected College Participants in a Higher Education Institution in the National Capital Region (NCR), Philippines" explores the factors that contribute to the initiations of e-cigarette usage among college students in the Philippines.

Riana's presentation in an international conference was unfortunately not shared by her university, University of Santo Tomas (UST), due to their unusually high standards of only posting those who have placed 1st in a category. Still, Riana is very proud of the opportunity to present the study to panelists and attendees in Yokohama, Japan.
